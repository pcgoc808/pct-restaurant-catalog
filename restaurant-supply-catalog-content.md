## DESIGN NOTES FOR CANVA

**Brand Colors:** Use PCT brand colors (pull from paradisecitytrading.com)
**Product Images:** Download from paradisecitytrading.com product pages or Shopify admin
**Layout:** Clean, professional wholesale catalog style. Grid layout for products. Large product photos where available.
**Font:** Modern, clean sans-serif
**Total Pages:** ~20 pages (cover + about + 16 product pages + back cover)
**Format:** Letter size (8.5x11) for print and digital PDF
**No prices on catalog** — separate price sheet will be provided
